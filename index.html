Our Story / हमारी कहानी

From 17 Beds to India's Largest Vermicompost Unit

एक सपने से शुरू हुई यात्रा, आज 20,000+ किसानों की पहचान

Dr. Sharvan Kumar Yadav holds a Ph.D. in Agronomy with specialization in Organic Farming from Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture & Technology (MPUAT), Udaipur. He worked as Senior Research Fellow at ICAR's All India Network Project on Organic Farming for 15+ years.

His father's cancer — caused by chemical-laden food — became the turning point. During the 2020 lockdown, Dr. Sharvan started with just 17 vermicompost beds. Today, his 5-hectare farm at Sundarpura, Jaipur runs 2500–2800 beds, producing 1500 tonnes per year.

He holds ASRB NET, ICAR JRF & SRF qualifications and has been mentored by Dr. S.K. Sharma, ADG (HRM), ICAR. His startup story was published by ICAR as a model for farmer innovation.
